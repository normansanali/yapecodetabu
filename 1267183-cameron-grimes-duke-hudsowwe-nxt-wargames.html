Cameron Grimes To Face Duke Hudson In Hair vs. Hair Match At WWE NXT WarGames

has a new attitude, and he might have a new look following WWE NXT WarGames if matters go Duke Hudson‘s way.

On the November 23 episode of WWE NXT 2.0, Grimes came out tearful and furious after Hudson did him dirty in last week’s Poker Showdown and explained to the crowd that while he’s made it in wrestling, he’s always kept his scraggly hair and beard as a reminder as to where he came from: rural North Carolina. These comments prompted Duke Hudson, who happened to be finishing up a haircut, to run down Grimes again. Hudson turned down Grimes’ challenge to a match until the former Million Dollar Champion called for a Hair vs. Hair match at NXT WarGames. After giving it some thought, Hudson accepted the challenge.

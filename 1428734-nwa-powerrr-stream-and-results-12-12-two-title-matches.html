NWA Powerrr Stream And Results (12/12): Two Title Matches

The National Wrestling Alliance presented an action-packed show with the December 12 episode of NWA Powerrr.

Among other matches, Colby Corino defended the NWA Junior Heavyweight Championship against Joe Ocasio. Plus, The Immortals defended the NWA U.S. Tag Team Championship against The Brothers of Funstruction.

The results are as follows:

  • Daisy Kill and Talos def. The Heavenly Butterflies
  • Backstage, NWA World Television Mims and Dak Draper preview their title match, which will be next week.
  • : Colby Corino (c) def. Joe Ocasio
  • Taylor Rising def. Airica Demia and Allie Recks
  • Backstage, The Southern Six preview their upcoming match against The Miserably Faithful.
  • In a video message, Matt Cardona challenged EC3 to a world title match at NWA Paranoia.
  • : The Brothers of Funstruction def. The Immortals (c) via DQ
    • A tag team named The Kids interfered and attacked The Brothers of Funstruction. The match ended in a disqualification. The Brothers of Funstruction and The Immortals brawled after the bell.

Adam Lambert &amp; Olly Alexander Bring Their Cool Style to Brit Awards 2021!

Adam Lambert and Olly Alexander both have very fun senses of style and they put their fashion-forward outfits on display at the 2021 Brit Awards!

The two singers met up on the red carpet at the event on Tuesday (May 11) at the O2 Arena in London, England.

Olly, who recently starred in the critically acclaimed HBO Max It’s a Sin, and his band Years & Years will be performing during the show with Elton John. If you didn’t know, Elton was a vocal supporter of the series and personally called up the cast to compliment their performances.

Adam presented the award for Best British Group alongside Olly Murs during the show.

Boy George also was on the red carpet and he just had dinner with Adam and his boyfriend a couple of weeks ago!

Pose actor Billy Porter also appeared on the red carpet. Check out photos in the gallery!

FYI: Adam is wearing Dolce & Gabbana. Olly is wearing Gucci. Billy is wearing a ThreeASFOUR look, an Oscar de la Renta bag, and Rick Owens boots.

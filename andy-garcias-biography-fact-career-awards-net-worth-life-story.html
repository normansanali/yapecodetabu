Andy Garcias biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

  • Source of Wealth: Film and Television
  • Age: 63
  • Birth Place: Havana, Cuba
  • Height: 5′ 10″ (1.78 m)
  • Weight: 79 kg (174 lbs)
  • Marital Status: Married (Maria Victoria Lorido)
  • Full Name: Andrés Arturo García Menéndez
  • Nationality: Cuban-American
  • Date of Birth: April 12, 1956
  • Ethnicity: Cuban and Spanish
  • Occupation: Actor
  • Education: Florida International University
  • Children: 4 (Dominik, Daniella, Andres and Alessandra)

Andres Arturo Garcia Menendez, better known just as Andy Garcia, was born on 12th April 1956 in Havana, Cuba. He is an American-Cuban actor with an estimated net worth of $20 million. Garcia is an actor, director and also a producer. His wealth comes from all these sources. He became famous by acting in movies like “The Mean Streets,” “The Untouchables,” and of course “The Godfather.”

Andy has been performing in both movies and television shows for a long time. He started his film career in 1978. Initially, Andy was seen on television, but later he began doing movies too. Now, he does both regularly. He started his film career by doing smaller roles in For Love and Honor, Brothers, Hill Street Blues, Murder She Wrote and A Night in Heaven. His role in “God Father:  Part 3” as Vincent Mancini became very famous. After that, he acted in several movies such as Hero, Dead Again, When a man loves a woman, Dangerous Minds, Night Falls on Manhattan and many more. His television performances are in Archie Bunker’s Place, The New Alfred Hitchcock Presents, For Love or Country: The Arturo Sandoval Story, Will & Grace, Kurtlar Vadisi and Top Gear among others.

Garcia has also directed and co-written a movie “The Lost City” in which he has also worked as an actor. Andy did the last episode of ‘Kurtlar Vadisi’ a Turkish television series. Over the years, Garcia has won many awards such as the ALMA Award for Outstanding Actor in a Feature Film, the Anthony Quinn Award for achievement in the motion picture, and the Nostros Golden Eagle Award for outstanding performer in a film.

Andy Garcia is married to Maria Victoria Lorido. The couple is blessed with four children.

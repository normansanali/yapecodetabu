Angelina Pivarnick calls cops on fianc Vinny Tortorella

Angelina Pivarnick’s engagement to Vinny Tortorella is off to a rocky start.

The “Jersey Shore” star called the cops to her and her fiancé’s home in Freehold, NJ, after an alleged altercation between the two, Page Six can exclusively reveal.

A police report obtained by Page Six shows Pivarnick dialed 911 after an alleged instance of domestic violence; however, upon the authorities’ arrival, she declined to press charges against her beau.

A FOIA request also said a “criminal investigatory record” was made in light of the alleged altercation.

“I can confirm that Angelina did in fact call the police to her home. After speaking with the officers, Angelina decided not to file charges,” Pivarnick’s attorney, James Leonard Jr., told Page Six in a statement Wednesday.

“This matter is now closed. Moving forward, Angelina would ask for privacy as it pertains to her personal life.”

Tortorella’s attorney, Elizabeth Rozin-Golinder, also told Page Six that he and Pivarnick are still engaged despite the incident.

“My client and Ms. Pivarnick are still engaged and are still residing together. Mr. Tortorella denies any act of impropriety that would warrant police involvement and denies any act of domestic violence occurred,” Rozin-Golinder said in a statement.

“No charges have been pressed against my client by Ms. Pivarnick or law enforcement. I ask that his privacy be respected in this regard.”

A source also tells Page Six that the couple is currently filming the MTV reality show’s next season.

“Angelina and Vinny are still engaged and are still very much in love with one another and they are both actively filming ‘Jersey Shore’ Season 7 that will air in the fall,” our source says.

Tortorella and Pivarnick, 37, became engaged during the latter’s “divorce” party honoring her the finalization of her split from her first husband, Chris Larangeira.

Are Wade and Bum Phillips Related?

The relationship between Wade and Bum Phillips is an intriguing tale of familial ties and a shared passion for American football.

Wade Phillips is the son of former NFL coach Bum Phillips, and their connection runs deep within the realms of football history. 

Wade Phillips: The Legacy Continues

Wade's adoration for his father is evident in his statements, where he expresses his gratitude for having Bum as both a father and a coach, attributing his coaching knowledge and moral compass to his father's teachings.

Wade's journey in football began at Port Neches–Groves High School, where he excelled as the quarterback of the football team, while his wife, Laurie, was the head cheerleader.

Wade's professional coaching career commenced with the Houston Oilers, where his father was the head coach.

He then ventured into various coaching roles, including defensive coordinator positions with the New Orleans Saints, Philadelphia Eagles, and the Denver Broncos.

Notably, he also served as the head coach for the Denver Broncos, Buffalo Bills, Dallas Cowboys, and the Houston Roughnecks.

Oail Andrew "Bum" Phillips, an esteemed former NFL coach, left an indelible mark on the sport.

Wes Phillips, the son of Wade Phillips, has also made significant contributions to the sport as an offensive coordinator and tight ends coach for various NFL teams.

Who Is Brendon Uries Wife, Sarah? Where Is She Now?

Brendon Urie’s wife, Sarah Urie, is also known for her acting and social media profession. 

When Brendon Urie saw Sarah Urie at his concert, it was love at first sight. After asking around to find out more about her, he eventually approached her and asked her out on a date.

Initially, Sarah wasn’t single, so they made plans to catch up when the time was right. Months passed, and Sarah was invited by Hayley Williams of Paramore to another Panic! At The Disco concert, and finally, Brendon and Sarah had their chance.

They have been married for a while and have solidified their love, which is evident through their social media posts showing the deep connection between them.

After more than ten years, the “High Hopes” singer revealed that he and Sarah are expecting their first child.

This was the conclusion of their four-year courtship, which had started with the sweetest love story. Despite having both purchased a home in Los Angeles, California, and riding off into the sunset together to start their happy union.

On January 24th of this year, Brendon took the band’s Instagram to share some heartbreaking news; he sadly announced that Panic! At The Disco’s career is officially ending due to their baby’s arrival. 

Even though it marks the end of an era for fans who’ve been embracing and following Brendon’s A-list music career over the years – it would appear that family life awaits him now more than ever with his two most important loves at his side: Sarah and their upcoming little one.

About Sarah Urie

Born in Detroit, Michigan on February 23, 1987, Sarah Urie eventually relocated to Los Angeles and found fame through her relationship with Brendon Urie. 

She began as an actor and esthetician before quickly achieving success with roles in films such as 2011’s “Good Cops“, becoming a notable figure in the entertainment world.

Now 35 years old, she stands 5 feet 8 inches tall and has a brother named Steve Orzechowski. 

Her presence on social media has skyrocketed over recent years, gaining her legions of admirers who love her engaging posts and snippets into her life as Mrs. Urie.

Sarah Urie has amassed more than 626k Instagram followers on her Official Instagram account @sarahurie.

Brian Regan Announces 2017 Tour Dates and Two Netflix Standup Specials

Brian Regan is having a very busy 2017. Today Regan announced a pile of standup tour dates, which run from early June through December and include stops in San Antonio, Denver, Wilmington, Orlando, San Diego, Madison, and more. In addition to the tour dates, Regan has landed two standup specials at Netflix, and he’ll tape the first special on tour in Denver on June 23rd and 24th. Check out the full list of tour dates below, and head over to Regan’s website for tickets.

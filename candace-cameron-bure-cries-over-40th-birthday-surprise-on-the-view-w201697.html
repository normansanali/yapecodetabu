Candace Cameron Bure Cries Over 40th Birthday Surprise on 'The View'

Full house of love! Candace Cameron Bure was the center of attention while celebrating her 40th birthday on The View on Wednesday, April 6.

The one-hour show was filled with surprise after surprise, which left the Fuller House star both overwhelmed and emotional. Cameron Bure’s cohosts — Whoopi Goldberg, Raven-Symoné, Joy Behar and Paula Faris — brought out a birthday cake that featured photos of her as a child and also shared videos of her Full House costars Bob Saget and Dave Coulier sending their well-wishes.

But the biggest shocker of all? Bure completely lost it (those tears!) when her 17-year-old daughter, Natasha, and For King & Country performed the band’s song “Priceless.” Cameron Bure covered her face and broke down in tears while sitting in the audience.

“Oh, my gosh. Oh, my gosh. Oh, my gosh!” the Dancing With the Stars alum said after running to the stage. The mother-daughter duo were then joined by their whole family — Cameron Bure’s husband, Valeri Bure, and the couple’s sons, Lev, 16, and Maksim, 14. “We’re good secret keepers,” Natasha said.

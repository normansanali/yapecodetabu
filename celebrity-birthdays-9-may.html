Celebrity birthdays 9 May: Billy Joel, Audrina Patridge and Rosario Dawson

A warm happy birthday to everyone who is celebrating today (9 May). Here at HELLO!, we are taking a look at some of the famous faces who will be toasting their birthdays with their loved ones. From a Hollywood actress to a legendary singer to a reality TV queen, scroll down to see who's celebrating…

Billy Joel turns 69

Happy birthday to the Piano Man chart-topper! The singer will no doubt be spoilt rotten by his wife Alexis Roderick, who he married in 2015, and their young daughters – two-year-old Della and baby Remy, who is only six months old. Billy also has an older daughter with his ex-wife Christie Brinkley, 32-year-old Alexa Ray, who announced her engagement at the start of the year.

Audrina Patridge turns 33

Many happy returns to The Hills alumn! The model and actress shot to fame after being cast in the reality TV series, starring alongside Lauren Conrad, Heidi Montag, and Whitney Port. Fans will also remember Audrina competing in Dancing with the Stars and having supporting roles in Honey 2 and Scary Movie 5. In 2016, the American star married husband Corey Bohan, but left him after ten months. Audrina shares a daughter Kirra with her ex.

Rosario Dawson turns 39

Some of her most famous credits include Rent, Sin City, Seven Pounds and Men in Black II, but the Hollywood actress has most recently starred as Claire Temple in five of the Marvel/Netflix shows: Daredevil, Jessica Jones, Luke Cage, Iron Fist, and The Defenders. The mother-of-one, who adopted her daughter in 2014, also played lawyer Jane Ramos in Jane the Virgin.

Christina Weiss Lurie proceeded to study at the Webber Douglas Academy of Dramatic Art in England, where she eventually dropped out. She later moved to Paris but headed to Los Angeles in an attempt to pursue a career in cinema and had worked for Aspect Ratio’s Ron Molder, an independent film production company.

Christina Weiss Lurie is currently the president of the Eagles Youth Partnership, and co-founder of three independent film companies including Vox3 Films, Tango Pictures and Screen Pass Pictures.

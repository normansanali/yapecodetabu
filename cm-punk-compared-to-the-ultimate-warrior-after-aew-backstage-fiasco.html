CM Punk Compared to The Ultimate Warrior After AEW Backstage Fiasco

CM Punk’s reputation precedes himself as he has managed to destroy his reputation in the span of just one year. All of this resulted in him getting fired from AEW last week and it appears Kevin Nash compared him to The Ultimate Warrior.

CM Punk has never failed to burn bridges with many companies he has worked with, as controversy always follows him every step of the way. He was fired from AEW last week after a lot of drama and now reports have suggested he wants to return to WWE.

The Second City Saint is branded as cancer in the pro wrestling business. While speaking on his Klip This podcast, WWE Hall of Famer Kevin Nash was asked about Punk returning to WWE. Nash compared CM Punk to The Ultimate Warrior after all the AEW backstage drama he caused.

He’s already put himself on the mantle right next to The Ultimate Warrior. Go shake the ropes buddy, you punched Luke Perry’s son, sucker punching his son.

Ringside News also exclusively reported that CM Punk wants fans to believe WWE is an option for him. CM Punk chants also broke out during the WWE Superstar Spectacle event. We will have to wait and see what will become of the Second City Saint in the end.

Galantis Announce Brooklyn Warehouse Show For Halloween

Recommended Videos

Halloween is just a couple of weeks away and already we’ve seen some pretty great show announcements for the Holiday weekend. East Coasters seem to have been particularly lucky this year with plenty of DJs headed to the big apple for the spookiest day of the year, with Above & Beyond having recently announced their Pier of Fear show, while Oliver Heldens teamed up with We Got This Covered for a ticket giveaway to his HI-LO-WEEN performance. Now, Galantis have announced their upcoming gig in Brooklyn for Halloween weekend.

The Swedish duo shared an image with their familiar seafox icon decked out with a jack-o-lanter’s smile, cluing fans in to their upcoming gig at the end of the month. For Halloween weekend, Galantis will be heading to New York to take over Brooklyn Warehouse on October 29 for a night of dance music insanity.

Galantis‘ Brookyln Warehouse show will be an eighteen and up affair, and is sure to be a memorable way to celebrate Halloween. If you’re in the mood for a good time, get your best rave friendly costume together and head here to purchase tickets to the event.

Gerard Butler - Biography

The Scottish actor was born in Renfrewshire and is best known for starring in The Phantom of the Opera, 300, How to Train Your Dragon, and more

    Gerard Butler's Early Life

    Gerard James Butler was born on November 13, 1969 in Paisley, Renfrewshire, Scotland. His family moved to Montreal, Canada when he was six months old, but when his parents split, he moved back to Scotland with his mother.

    Butler attended and graduated from law school, spending a year in California before his final lap, and aimed to become a lawyer. However, problems with alcohol and a lack of discipline prevented him from reaching that goal, he moved to London, where he worked a series of odd jobs with the intent of "becoming famous."

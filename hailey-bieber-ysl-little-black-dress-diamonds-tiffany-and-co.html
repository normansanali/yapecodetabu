Hailey Bieber Is Striking in a Little Black Dress and Strings of Diamonds

Hailey Bieber is here to prove that a classic, timeless look can still make the best-dressed list at a fashion party. The supermodel was the definition of sophistication last night while attending a lavish bash in Miami.

She made an appearance at the Tiffany & Co. Miami Holiday Residence fete and looked stunning in a minimalistic black look from Saint Laurent by Anthony Vaccarello—drenched in diamonds from Tiffany & Co.

Tiffany & Co./BFA

Bieber's LBD is Saint Laurent's shiny draped jersey dress; a true It-girl piece, it features a high neck, long sleeves, and a flower decoration at the front that falls into a long tail, contrasting the short hemline of the skirt.

True to her clean-girl aesthetic, Bieber paired the dress with the brand's silver slingback pumps and accessorized with layers of Tiffany & Co. diamond jewelry: the Tiffany Victoria Line Bracelet, Mixed Cluster Earrings, Vine Necklace, and Graduated Line Necklace.

The Rhode founder has become known for her effortlessly chic style, often taking inspiration from style icons who came before her, including Grace Kelly and Princess Diana.

In Harper's BAZAAR's September 2022 cover story, the Saint Laurent darling spoke earnestly about her attitude toward dressing. "I get photographed so much that I feel like sometimes I put pressure on myself," she said. "Even if I'm just throwing on jeans and a T-shirt, I want it to be a dope pair of jeans and a great T-shirt!"

Bieber's Miami visit comes amid Art Basel and Miami Art Week, two annual events dedicated to celebrating the arts, including fashion, in the city.

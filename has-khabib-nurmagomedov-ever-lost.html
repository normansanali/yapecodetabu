Has Khabib Nurmagomedov ever lost?

Khabib Nurmagomedov, also known as "The Eagle," is a retired mixed martial artist who is widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters in the history of the sport. Born on September 20, 1988, in the Republic of Dagestan, Russia, Khabib had an illustrious career in the UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship), where he held the lightweight title from April 2018 to March 2021. Throughout his professional career, Khabib achieved an impressive undefeated record which has left many wondering, has he ever lost?

The Undefeated Streak

Khabib Nurmagomedov holds a perfect professional fighting record of 29 wins and 0 losses. This undefeated streak is a remarkable achievement in a sport as demanding and unpredictable as mixed martial arts (MMA). Khabib's dominance in the lightweight division was unparalleled, and he often showcased his incredible grappling skills and relentless ground and pound to overcome his opponents.

The Early Years

Khabib's journey in MMA began in 2008 when he made his professional debut in the sport. He quickly gained attention for his explosive style and dominant performances. In his early years, Khabib faced several tough opponents, but he emerged victorious in every bout. His ability to control his opponents on the ground and impose his will on them became his trademark.

The UFC Career

Khabib Nurmagomedov signed with the UFC in 2012, and it was here that he truly began to make his mark on the sport. He faced a series of formidable opponents and dispatched them with relative ease. Some of his notable victories include wins over Conor McGregor, Dustin Poirier, Rafael dos Anjos, and Michael Johnson.

The Conor McGregor Fight

One of the most highly anticipated fights in MMA history was the clash between Khabib Nurmagomedov and Conor McGregor in October 2018. McGregor was a former two-division champion and a polarizing figure in the sport. The buildup to the fight was marred by controversy and personal animosity between the two fighters.

When the fight finally took place, Khabib showcased his dominance once again. He controlled McGregor throughout the fight, eventually submitting him in the fourth round with a neck crank. However, the fight was marred by a post-fight brawl that broke out between the two fighters' camps, tarnishing the victory somewhat.

The Retirement

After his victory over Justin Gaethje in October 2020, Khabib Nurmagomedov shocked the world by announcing his retirement from the sport. This decision came after the tragic passing of his father and coach, Abdulmanap Nurmagomedov. Khabib expressed his desire to honor his father's wish for him to retire undefeated.

Khabib Nurmagomedov has never lost a professional MMA fight throughout his career. His undefeated streak of 29 wins is a testament to his exceptional skill, discipline, and determination. Khabib's dominance in the lightweight division of the UFC was unmatched, and he defeated some of the best fighters in the world during his tenure. While there were moments of controversy and post-fight altercations, Khabib's legacy as an undefeated champion will forever be etched in the annals of MMA history.

How Much Does Adidas Pay Noah Lyles?

The Contract Details

Noah Lyles' contract with Adidas includes a partnership that runs until the end of the decade, encompassing major events such as the Paris 2024 and Los Angeles 2028 Olympic Games. As part of the deal, Lyles will continue to wear Adidas running shoes and other apparel, solidifying his association with the brand.

How to cancel fabletics subscription

Can I cancel my Fabletics account online?

There is no cancellation fee and you can cancel anytime. You may cancel your membership by calling one of our customer service representatives at 1-844-322-5384 (24 hours, 7 days a week). You may also cancel by contacting us via online live chat or by visiting your My Account page.

Is Fabletics easy to cancel?

You cannot cancel Fabletics online. Regardless of what you think of our Fabletics review below, please keep this part in mind. If you’re not a person who likes to deal with calling customer service, then this deal might not be for you, no matter how good you think it is.

Can I cancel Fabletics after first order?

Your VIP membership can be canceled at any time by calling 1-844-Fabletics (1-844-322-5384).

How do I cancel my Fabletics month?

When you visit the site during this time period, there will be a window on your homepage that says “skip this month”—follow the prompts and you will have successfully skipped the month. You can also call our customer service number at 1-844-322-5384 (24 hours, 7 days a week) to skip the month as well.

Can you skip every month on Fabletics?

If you‘re using our mobile app, tap on the “My Account” tab and then “Account Info.” Scroll to the bottom of the page to skip the month and you won’t be charged a Member Credit. Even if you skip the month, you can still make a purchase whenever you want.

Does Fabletics charge you every month?

How Much Does the Fabletics VIP Membership Cost? After your initial purchase as a VIP member, you‘ll be eligible to purchase an outfit (top, bottom and/or sports bra) starting at $49.95 each, every month. There’s no cost to become a VIP member, but you must take an action every month — buy an outfit or skip the month.

Is the Fabletics membership worth it?

Is Fabletics worth it? If you plan to use your credit every month, a Fabletics VIP Membership provides a good price on quality leggings. However, if you’re unsure that you would use the account credit each month, a subscription-based model might not be for you.

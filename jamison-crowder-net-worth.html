Jamison Crowder Net Worth

Jamison Wesley Crowder is an American football wide receiver for the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League (NFL). A native of Monroe, North Carolina, Crowder was a three-year letterman at Monroe High School under coach Johnny Sowell.

Crowder played college football at Duke University from 2011 to 2014 under head coach David Cutcliffe. As a true freshman, he played in all 12 games and recorded 14 receptions for 163 yards and one touchdown.

Crowder was picked by the Washington Redskins in the fourth round with the 105th overall pick of the 2015 NFL Draft. He signed a four-year rookie contract on May 11, 2015. On March 22, 2022, Crowder signed with the Buffalo Bills on a one-year deal.

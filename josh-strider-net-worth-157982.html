About

Top 10 list video creator whose listicle content focuses on cartoon television series. Some of the most watched videos on his PhantomStrider channel include "Top 10 Worst Cartoon Network Shows" and "Top 10 Worst Modern Cartoons." Contrary to the aforementioned videos, he does post "Top 10 Best" lists as well. 

Before Fame

He was a signal intelligence officer in the Navy for one year. He studied psychology at Queensland University of Technology beginning in March of 2014. 

Trivia

His PhantomStrider YouTube channel has accrued 370,000 subscribers. He has a secondary channel called LiveStrider to publish more lists as well as Let's Plays. He uses an avatar of a South Park character to represent himself. 

Family Life

Associated With

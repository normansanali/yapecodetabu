Wiki/Biography

Shantinath Sul a.k.a Just Sul was born on June 1, 1969 (age 51 years; as in 2020). He was born and raised in Mumbai and he completed his education with a bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering. He then moved to Saudi Arabia to work in the field, and later he was asked to Zambia, Central Africa where he worked with his father’s friend. In 2015, he uploaded his first video, and since then he has been working as an engineer, as well as, a content creator.

Just Sul is of Indian nationality as he was born and brought up in India. He got married when he was in his 20s.

Wife and children

Just Sul is married and his family lives in India. He has a son and a daughter and the name of his wife and children are not known. His son is pursuing electrical engineering and his daughter is pursuing architecture.

Career

Just Sul started his journey as a mechanical engineer from Saudi Arabia and then moved to Africa to work in his father’s friend’s company. There he met his son, Said Ahmad, who saw potential in Sul and asked Sul if he wanted to be famous. The whole idea of Said was to mix Sul’s Indian heritage with the hip hop culture and this lead to the creation of Just Sul. Sul is known for recreating celebrity photoshoots and recording funny videos. He uploaded his first video in April 2015 on Vine and the response on the video was overwhelming. Said decided to move his work to YouTube and Instagram and now he has a fan base of more than 6 million people. His videos were appreciated by several celebrities as well.

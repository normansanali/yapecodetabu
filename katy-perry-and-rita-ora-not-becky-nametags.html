Katy Perry and Rita Ora Wore Not Becky Name-tags To The Met Gala

Most guests accessorized their Met Gala looks last night with sequins, robot-inspired accents or you know, actual jewelry, but Katy Perry and Rita Ora opted to take a more Beyoncé-inspired route.

The two singers showed up to last night's after-parties wearing "Not Becky" name tags to help get us all one step closer to answering the greatest question of our time: who is Becky with the good hair? 

Elsewhere at the Met Gala after-parties, Beyoncé was busy not caring at all about any of our Becky with the good hair woes.

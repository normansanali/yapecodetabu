Kylie Jenner Debuts New Pink Hair on Instagram

They say what comes around goes around — and even though that reference isn't specifically in reference to Kylie Jenner's changing hairstyles, it's definitely appropriate. Today, the mom and mogul shared a carousel of snapshots on Instagram that showed her with pink hair again after the reality star dabbled in the quiet luxury trend last year and had her hair in a more natural dark chocolate shade. Of course, Jenner has had just about every single hair color under the planet, but the shift back to pink is something fans are calling a throwback to the days of King Kylie.

"Hiiiii remember me," she captioned the trio of images, which showed her in her car wearing a simple ribbed gray tank top. She paired the pink transformation with a subtle ombré lip combination and long lashes. 

Back in 2017, Jenner had pink hair that she dubbed rosé. In the years prior to that, she was famous for experimenting with wigs and colors that ranged from blonde and bronde to neon wigs and even blue. 

Jenner opened up about her penchant for switching things up back in 2015, revealing that the first time that she did it, it was without her mother Kris Jenner's permission.

“After that, I’ve just had this addiction to changing my hair. It makes me feel like a new person. I love feeling different, and I love leaving the house knowing that nobody has ever seen me this way,” she wrote at the time. She also noted that she isn't one for keeping to one look for long. That's proven to be true in the years since the statement. “It’s hard for me to go back to different styles I’ve worn before; I don’t always like my normal short, black hair. That look makes me feel like I’m going back in time. It’s like I’m going back to that moment a year ago, and I just don’t want to be in that space.”

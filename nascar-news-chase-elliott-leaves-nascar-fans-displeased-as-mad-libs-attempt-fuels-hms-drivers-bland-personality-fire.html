The Controversial 'Mad Libs' Initiative

Hendrick Motorsports Launched A Social Media Initiative Called 'Mad Libs,' Aiming To Create A Light-Hearted Connection Between Its Drivers And Fans. However, When It Was Chase Elliott's Turn, His Seemingly Disinterested Demeanor Left Fans Questioning His Enthusiasm.

Elliott's Unusual Answers

Elliott, A Six-Time Winner Of The NMPA Most Popular Driver Of The Year Award, Was Asked About His Preferences, Including His Favorite Food And Race Track. His Responses, However, Were Perceived As Lackluster, Leading Some Fans To Suggest That Fellow Driver Alex Bowman Might Have Been A Better Fit For The Video.

A Detour to Remember

Among The More Memorable Moments Was When Elliott Shared A Story About A Trip That Was Supposed To End In Bristol But Instead Took Him To New York. He Even Ran Into Former HMS Driver And 7-time Champion Jimmie Johnson At A Hooters Outlet. Despite The Amusing Anecdote, Fans Were Disappointed With Elliott's Overall Performance In The Video.

Nina Dobrev Flirts With Tim Tebow on 'Lip Sync Battle'

Ni-Na! In a new preview from the upcoming episode of Lip Sync Battle, Vampire Diaries alum Nina Dobrev competes against Tim Tebow, but their face-off turns flirty as she performs Marvin Gaye‘s classic “Let’s Get It On.”

In the clip, the 27-year-old beauty takes to the stage in a leather jacket and skinny jeans, and gets very into the soulful, sexy tune as she lip syncs solo. Then the actress decides she needs a partner, and casually walks over to an unsuspecting Tebow, 28.

Dobrev drops to the floor and slowly dances up against the former NFL player. Meanwhile, a flustered Tebow looks down and laughs as she continues to work her moves.

Spike released a preview of Tebow’s performance this past Monday, and it’s just as good. In it, the 2007 Heisman Trophy winner channels Rocky Balboa in his performance of “Eye of the Tiger.”

Paris Jackson Net Worth


Paris Jackson is the only daughter of the late pop music icon Michael Jackson. She was born on April 3, 1998, in Beverly Hills, California, and inherited a net worth of $100 million from her father after his passing in 2009. Despite not having achieved significant accomplishments in the entertainment industry, she has amassed considerable wealth, mainly through her father’s inheritance.

Growing up, Paris and her brothers had to wear masks to avoid being recognized in public due to the high-profile status of their father. Following Michael Jackson’s death, Paris and her brothers were placed under the legal guardianship of their grandmother, Katherine Jackson, and have since appeared in various public events and TV shows.

Despite facing challenges, such as bullying and the pressure of being in the spotlight, Paris has ventured into acting, modeling, and television appearances, which have contributed to her net worth. She has also faced personal struggles, including battles with depression and suicide attempts, but has since recovered and is pursuing a career in the entertainment industry.

Paris Jackson’s public appearances and achievements, combined with her family’s legacy, have continued to contribute to her net worth, making her a prominent figure in the entertainment industry.

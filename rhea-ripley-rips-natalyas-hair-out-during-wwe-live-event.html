Rhea Ripley Rips Natalyas Hair Out During WWE Live Event

Rhea Ripley has worked very hard to become one of the top female Superstars in WWE. She dedicated numerous years to the pro wrestling business, which can often be very thankless in many ways. Now it appears she ripped Natalya’s hair out during a recent WWE live event.

Rhea Ripley has defeated numerous competitors since becoming the WWE Women’s Champion earlier this year. This included Natalya, with whom she had a stellar match on RAW earlier this month.

Nattie challenged Rhea Ripley for the Women’s World Championship at WWE Supershow in Fairfax. The Judgment Day member ripped a piece of Natalya’s hair off during the match and even twirled it around, to add insult to injury.

The Eradiactor also used the hair locks to disgust the match official into stepping out of her way. Naturally, Ripley won the match and successfully retained her title. Regardless, we’ll have to wait and see what WWE will have planned for Rhea Ripley as we head towards the WWE SummerSlam Premium Live Event.

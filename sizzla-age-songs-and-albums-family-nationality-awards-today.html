Sizzla Age, Songs and Albums, Family, Nationality, Awards, Today

Stagenamed, Sizzla Kalonji, born Miguel Orlando Collins is a reggae artist from the land of enjoyment, Jamaica.

Sizzla Age

How old is Sizzla? Sizzla is 46 years old in 2022. Sizzla was born on April 17, 1976.

Sizzla Songs and Album

With a career spanning over 32 years, Sizzla has released a lot of albums and songs. Sizzla’s songs include ‘Just One Of Those Days’, ‘Thank You Mamma’, ‘Solid as Rock’, ‘Give me a Try’, ‘Be Strong’, ‘Guide Over Us’ and many more.

Sizzla’s albums include ‘I-Space’, ‘I’m Yours’, ‘In Gambia’, ‘Black History’, ‘Jah Knows Best’, ‘Taking Over’, ‘Freedom Cry’, ‘Praise Ye Jah’, ‘Da Real Thing’, ‘Burning Up’ and many more.

Sizzla Family

Sizzla was born and raised in Kingston, Jamaica by his Rastafarian Parents. Information on Sizzla having a sibling is unknown.

Sizzla Nationality

Sizzla is from Jamaica.

Sizzla Awards

Sizzla has won many awards from quite a lot of nominations including the Icon Culture in the International Reggae and World Music Awards (IRAWMA) and a Grammy Award for best reggae album.

The Upton Brothers: A Tale of Two MLB Stars for Justin and B.J

By Jocelyn Alano June 27, 2024 16:45

credit. Kevin C. Cox/Getty Images)

Both brothers, Justin and BJ, born and raised in Norfolk, Virginia came from modest backgrounds as their father Melvin is a scout for the Kansas City Royals while their mother Yvonne serves as a teacher. The environment they were raised in provided the strong background of their work ethics and similarly, both started a successful career within Major League Baseball (MLB).


Childhood and early baseball success

Born on August 21, 1984, B.J. Upton, also known as Melvin Emanuel Upton Jr., began his journey in baseball at an early age. He attended Greenbrier Christian Academy in Chesapeake, Virginia, where he honed his skills and demonstrated exceptional talent on the field. His innate ability led to numerous accolades during his high school career, including being named a first-team All-American by Baseball America in 2002. Just a few years later, on August 25, 1987, Justin Irvin Upton was born, and much like his older brother, he exhibited an extraordinary aptitude for baseball from a young age. In 2005, he was selected first overall by the Arizona Diamondbacks in the MLB draft, marking the beginning of his professional baseball journey.


MLB Journey & Brotherly Bonds

B.J. Upton made his MLB debut on August 2, 2004, with the Tampa Bay Devil Rays and went on to play for five different teams over a thirteen-year career (Tampa Bay Devil Rays /Ray, Atlanta Braves, Pittsburgh Pirates, San Diego Padres, Toronto Blue Jays). He and Justin are the only brothers in MLB history to be selected with the first and second picks of just about any draft.

Brotherly Love

Easily, one of the more fun angles about their baseball odyssey is how they have gotten to play on teams together - making for pieces of history in this wonderful game. Together they visited the Atlanta Braves and San Diego Padres entrenched enough as bro-bros in baseball for at least one legendary summer, two men whose inherent talents created chemistry despite what had been a fractured connection since they were kids.


Enduring Legacy and Impact

However, it is more than just what the Upton brothers have done individually; their MLB footprint goes much further. Their story captures the essence of resilience, and commitment and provides a heartwarming tale about sibling love. Their mark on the sport will last through multiple generations, inspiring future baseball players to chase their dreams armed with nothing more than a dash of talent an ocean full of hard work and unwavering family support.
The Upton brothers' path from youth to professional baseball super-stardom is a testament to their exceptional ability, unparalleled dedication, and the indelible bond of brotherhood.

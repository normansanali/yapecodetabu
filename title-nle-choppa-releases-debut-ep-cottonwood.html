NLE Choppa 'Cottonwood' EP Stream, Cover Art &amp; Tracklist

NLE Choppa closes out his breakthrough year with his first studio project, Cottonwood. The rising star’s EP is named after the block in Memphis where he grew up and later got tattooed on his arm.

The 17-year-old artist’s EP serves as a prelude to his debut album, which is expected to drop in the first quarter of 2020. The project is comprised of nine tracks, including his hit single “Shotta Flow” and the track’s official remix featuring Blueface.

Check out Choppa’s Cottonwood stream, cover art and tracklist below.

1. Untold
2. Step
3. Side
4. Clicc Clacc
5. Matrix
6. N.W.A.
7. Chances
8. Shotta Flow
9. Shotta Flow (Remix) f. Blueface

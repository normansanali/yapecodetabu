Where did Leonard Fournette go to college and high school?

Leonard Fournette is an American football running back for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers of the National Football League. He was drafted by the Jacksonville Jaguars with the fourth overall pick in the 2017 NFL Draft. He is the son of  Leonard Jr. and his wife Lory. He was born to his parents on January 18, 1995, in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States. He is currently at the age of 27 and stands tall at a height of 1.83 m.

He has been on the news after Buccaneers coaches reportedly not happy with him weighing roughly 260 pounds. Having said that, in this article, we will dive into his personal life to know more about him, especially his educational background to know where he attended high school and college.

Where did Leonard Fournette go to college?

He is a graduate of Louisiana State University. He enrolled in the University in 2014 when he announced his decision to attend the University at the 2014 Under Armour All-America Game.

He played college football for the LSU Tigers.

Where did Leonard Fournette go to high school?

Leonard attended St. Augustine High School –  a private, Catholic, all-boys high school run by the Josephites in New Orleans, Louisiana. He played football in high school and competed in track and field.

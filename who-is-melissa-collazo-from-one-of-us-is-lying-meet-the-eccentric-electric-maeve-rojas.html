Who Is Melissa Collazo From One Of Us Is Lying? Meet The Eccentric &amp; Electric Maeve Rojas

Melissa Collazo is a prominent actress known for her role in the Tv series, One of Us Is Lying.

Melissa Collazo has made her name in the entertainment industry by portraying various roles in TV series and movies. She got noticed for her role as Maeve Rojas in the Tv series, One of Us is Lying.

One of Us is Lying series is based on the novel of the same name where five students at Bayview High School walk into detention, and only four come out alive.

Who Is Melissa Collazo From One Of Us Is Lying? Wikipedia

Melissa Collazo is famously known as the Maeve Rojas from One of Us is Lying, and she is yet to have her own Wikipedia page. She was in the entertainment industry from a young age as she grew up on news sets with her parents.

Melissa got her first gig at age 5 when an entertainment reporter spotted her. At the age of 8, she was a kid reporter who hosted an entertainment segment for Telemundo Network and did it until she was about 12.

She was inspired into acting by the Disney Channel’s show, Hannah Montana, and at the age of 16, she got her first agent. Her career started to move forward and started to book jobs shortly.

Melissa landed her first role in the indie film Lena and Snowball, where she played the lead role of Lena. Similarly, she also was seen in the DCEU TV Series Swamp Thing.

Actress Melissa Collazo Age Details

Melissa Collazo is 21 years old and was born on July 15, 2000, in Orlando, Florida. She completed her high school education at Richmond Hill High School.

Melissa has been portrayed as Rob’s Student in Naked’s role, and she is also seen in the role of Arcade Gamer in the hit TV Series, Stranger Things in 2017.

Similarly, she was in the movie, The Little Mermaid in 2018 in the role of Circus Oddity. While her recent role is in the most talked-about drama, One of Us is Lying. Similarly, the series is getting its season 2, but the release date is yet to be fixed.

Meet Melissa Collazo Parents

Melissa Collazo was born to her Puerto Rican parents, due to which she is fluent in both English and Spanish. During an interview, she revealed that her mother was the news director at the Latin American station Telemundo.

Besides these details, there is not much information about her parents, and, shares hardly anything about them on her social media accounts.

Melissa is active on the social media platforms like Instagram and Twitter. She is active on Instagram as @melissacollazo and has 32.5k followers.

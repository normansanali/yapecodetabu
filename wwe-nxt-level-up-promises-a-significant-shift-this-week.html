WWE NXT Level Up Promises a Significant Shift This Week

WWE fans, we’ve got a bit of a throwback treat for you tonight! So, here’s the scoop: the WWE show scheduled for tonight, November 24, won’t be featuring fresh new matches. Nope, instead, they’re taking a little trip down memory lane by airing some classic bouts from earlier this year.

Now, let’s break it down. This past Tuesday’s episode of NXT was actually pre-taped a week ahead of time due to the Thanksgiving holiday. That meant they had to cram two back-to-back NXT TV tapings into one night last week. Phew, talk about a wrestling marathon!

But here’s the deal: due to the extended showtime for the fans in the arena, no additional matches were taped for NXT Level Up. Instead of leaving you hanging, WWE’s got your back and decided to serve up some nostalgia.

So, what’s on the menu? WWE has announced four matches from earlier in 2023 that’ll be making a comeback on tonight’s Level Up.

Here’s the lineup:

  • Eddy Thorpe vs. Dante Chen from February
  • Ivy Nile vs. Lola Vice from April
  • Wendy Choo vs. Kelani Jordan from May
  • Axiom vs. Tavion Heights from July

WWE is hyping this special best-of episode, promising to showcase some of the most memorable and exhilarating matches from the brand in 2023. It’s like a trip down memory lane for wrestling fans!

So, don’t miss your chance to relive all the magic of NXT Level Up on this special night. You can catch it streaming at 10/9 C on Peacock in the United States and on WWE Network everywhere else.

Level Up airs right after SmackDown every week, so make sure to tune in and enjoy the action-packed throwback!
